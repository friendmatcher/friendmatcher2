Connect Globally!

This friend matching program allows people all around the world to join large friend groups with just a click. Join us in connecting the world to bring cultural and social understanding and to stop the pain of loneliness.

The Staff

Sneh Girdhar and Anusha Vemuri are Amador Valley students who created this website to bring awareness to the opportunities that social networking provides. They envision of using this website as a hub for sharing ideas, paralleling great social media's (like Facebook and Twitter) influence.
